# Readout — 2-minute demo script

*(~250 words, ~2 min at normal pace)*

---

**0:00 — Hook**

"Founders waste weeks rewriting the same story for Reddit, email, and LinkedIn. Readout fixes that: one repo, one brief, channel-ready drafts in minutes."

---

**0:15 — Ingest**

"First, connect your repo. Point Readout at a GitHub repo — we pull the README, docs, whatever paths you choose. AI summarizes it into a product knowledge base so we're always drafting from your actual product, not a stale blurb. [Show repo connect / ingest screen.]"

---

**0:35 — Brief**

"Next, your brief. Who's your audience? What tone — casual, professional, technical? What are you trying to do — awareness, signups, feedback? Pick your channels: Reddit, email, LinkedIn. One brief drives everything. [Show brief form.]"

---

**0:50 — Generate**

"Hit generate. For Reddit we discover relevant subreddits and write posts that fit each one. For email you get subject lines and body copy. For LinkedIn, post-ready text. Everything stays on-brand because it's all coming from the same product knowledge and the same brief. [Show generated drafts.]"

---

**1:10 — Manage & polish**

"You get a list of drafts you can tweak, regenerate, or improve with AI. Re-sync from the repo whenever you ship — messaging stays aligned. Scheduling and publishing are on the roadmap. [Optional: show improve-draft or chat.]"

---

**1:25 — Close**

"Readout: unified outreach from your repo. Connect once, brief once, generate everywhere. Try it at [repo or demo URL]."

---

**Production notes**

- Show the app (frontend) for each step; keep backend/API off-camera unless it's a technical audience.
- If the UI isn't ready, use API/Postman or a simple CLI flow and say "here's the same flow via the API."
- End frame: logo + tagline + "Unified outreach from your repo."
